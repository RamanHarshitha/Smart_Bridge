About OptiCrop

Built for farmers, agronomists, researchers & policymakers.

The OptiCrop project revolutionizes agricultural practices by providing a smart production optimization engine. By integrating key environmental factors and crop-specific recommendations, OptiCrop enables farmers to achieve optimal yields, improve resource efficiency, and contribute to sustainable agricultural practices.

The knowledge and insights gained through OptiCrop can be utilized by agricultural researchers, policymakers, and stakeholders to advance the understanding of crop-environment interactions and inform the development of evidence-based agricultural strategies.
